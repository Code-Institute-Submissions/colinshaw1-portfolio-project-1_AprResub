Headspace is an app that has a paid subscription. The app will offer hundreds of guided meditations to help with everything from stress to focus to relationships. The sleep section has stories, soundscapes and music to help sleep soundly. Click on the mind icon beside the headspace heading to see for yourself.

Calm

Calm is an app that will improve your health and happiness. By improving sleep quality, reducing anxiety and stress, improving focus, and helping build and improve yourself. Click on the bed icon beside the headspace heading to see for yourself.
